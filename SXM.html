St. Maarten Brief History/Quick Facts
Flag Of Sint.Maarten
Map of Sint.Maarten
  1. St. Maarten was discovered by Christopher Columbus on November 11,1493.
  2. For the next 150 years St. Maarten was inhabited by the Netherlands, England,France and Spain. Finally in 1648 the French and Dutch signed the treaty of Concordia which divided the island in two
  3. Both a Frenchman and a Dutchman stood back to back and walked in opposite directions around the shoreline, drawing the boundary from the spot where they met thus leaving the French side with 21sqm and the Dutch side with 16sqm.
